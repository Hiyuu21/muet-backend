# 🎓 MUET Hub: AI-Powered Study Partner

MUET Hub is a full-stack web application designed to help Malaysian candidates prepare for the Malaysian University English Test (MUET). It leverages the Gemini 2.5 Flash API to automatically generate reading quizzes and grade writing essays from uploaded PDFs.

## ✨ Features
* **📖 AI Reading Quizzes:** Upload a MUET reading passage (PDF), and the AI automatically extracts the text and generates interactive multiple-choice questions.
* **✍️ Smart Essay Grader:** Practice Task 1 and Task 2 writing prompts. The AI evaluates your answer, predicts a MUET Band score, and provides actionable feedback on strengths and areas for improvement.
* **📂 Cloud Resource Library:** A centralized hub to view and download past-year papers and study notes, powered by secure cloud storage.
* **📱 Mobile Responsive:** Clean, modern UI that works perfectly on desktop and mobile devices.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
* **Database:** MongoDB Cloud (Atlas)
* **Cloud Storage:** Cloudinary (via Multer)
* **AI Integration:** Google Gemini 2.5 Flash API
* **Deployment:** Netlify (Frontend) & Render (Backend)

# 📘 MUET Hub: User Manual / Panduan Pengguna

Welcome to MUET Hub! This AI-powered tool is designed to help you score your best. Here is a quick guide on how to use the platform.
*Selamat datang ke MUET Hub! Alat berkuasa AI ini direka untuk membantu anda mendapat skor terbaik. Berikut adalah panduan ringkas cara menggunakan platform ini.*

⚠️ **Quick Note / Nota Ringkas:** If the site hasn't been used in the last 15 minutes, the AI engine goes to sleep. **If it takes 30-60 seconds to load your first quiz, don't worry! It is just waking up.**
*Jika laman web tidak digunakan dalam 15 minit yang lepas, enjin AI akan 'tidur'. **Jika ia mengambil masa 30-60 saat untuk memuatkan kuiz pertama anda, jangan risau! Ia sedang dihidupkan semula.***

---

### 📖 1. Reading Practice / Latihan Membaca
**EN:** 1. Navigate to the **Reading Practice** tab.
2. The AI will automatically load the latest MUET reading passage and questions.
3. Read the passage carefully, select your answers, and submit to see your score instantly!

**BM:**
1. Pergi ke tab **Reading Practice**.
2. AI akan memuatkan petikan dan soalan MUET terkini secara automatik.
3. Baca petikan dengan teliti, pilih jawapan anda, dan hantar untuk melihat markah anda serta-merta!

---

### ✍️ 2. Writing Grader / Penilai Penulisan
**EN:**
1. Navigate to the **Writing Practice** tab.
2. Read the generated MUET Task 1 or Task 2 prompt.
3. Type your essay into the text box provided (keep an eye on the word counter!).
4. Click "Submit" to let the AI grade your essay. You will receive an estimated Band Score, your strengths, and tips for improvement.

**BM:**
1. Pergi ke tab **Writing Practice**.
2. Baca soalan Task 1 atau Task 2 MUET yang dijana.
3. Taip esei anda ke dalam kotak teks yang disediakan (perhatikan pengira perkataan!).
4. Klik "Submit" untuk membiarkan AI menilai esei anda. Anda akan menerima anggaran Skor Band, kekuatan penulisan anda, dan tips untuk penambahbaikan.

---

### 📂 3. Notes & Resources / Nota & Sumber
**EN:**
1. Click on the **Notes & Resources** tab.
2. Here you will find past year papers, grammar notes, and tips.
3. Click "View / Download" on any card to save the PDF directly to your device. 

**BM:**
1. Klik pada tab **Notes & Resources**.
2. Di sini anda akan menemui kertas soalan tahun lepas, nota tatabahasa, dan tips.
3. Klik "View / Download" pada mana-mana kad untuk menyimpan fail PDF terus ke peranti anda.
